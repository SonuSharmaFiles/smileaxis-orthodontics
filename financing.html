Financing FAQ

Common questions about pricing and payment

Straight answers to what most patients ask us about financing.

Do you offer monthly payment plans?

Yes ‚Äî most patients use monthly plans. We offer in-house financing with no credit check required, plus third-party options with 0% interest for qualified patients. Plans start from $149/month and are reviewed at your free consultation after your 3D scan.

Do you accept dental insurance?

Yes. Many orthodontic plans include a lifetime benefit of $1,000‚Äì$3,000+. We verify your benefits before your first visit, bill insurance directly, and apply your benefit immediately to reduce your out-of-pocket amount. We work with most major PPO plans.

Can I use my HSA or FSA?

Yes ‚Äî orthodontic treatment is a qualified HSA and FSA expense. This is a great way to use pre-tax dollars. We'll confirm the eligible portion of your treatment at your consultation.

Is the initial consultation truly free?

100% free ‚Äî no strings attached. Your first visit includes a full orthodontic exam, a 3D iTero scan (valued at $350), and a personalized smile plan with timeline and all-in cost. No obligation to start treatment.

What happens if I need extra aligners mid-treatment?
