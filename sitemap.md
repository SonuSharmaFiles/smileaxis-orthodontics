## Why each page exists + how it supports conversions + SEO

Below is the functional intent for each page type. (In implementation, each page should end with “Book Consult” + contact options, and include FAQ schema where appropriate.)

### Homepage (`/`)
- **Why it exists:** The brand’s single strongest first impression. Consolidates premium positioning, authority, and convenience.
- **Conversion support:** Immediate scheduling CTA + fast trust proof.
- **SEO support:** Ranks for branded + broad terms over time; supports internal linking to the Invisalign/Braces hubs.

### Treatments hub & treatment pages

#### `/Treatments/Invisalign/` (Required)
- **Why:** Core revenue driver + high intent.
- **Conversion:** Candidate screening, “what you’ll pay / how it works,” schedule consult.
- **SEO:** Primary target: **“Invisalign Austin”**, plus long-tails (timeline, attachments, pain, provider).

#### `/Treatments/Braces/` (Required)
- **Why:** Captures comprehensive cases + parents/teens.
- **Conversion:** Braces options, timeline, comfort promise.
- **SEO:** Target: **“Braces Austin”**, “ceramic braces,” “orthodontic braces Austin.”

#### `/Treatments/Invisalign-vs-Braces/`
- **Why:** Decision-stage page; reduces friction.
- **Conversion:** Helps visitors choose and book consult.
- **SEO:** Targets comparison queries and captures uncertain traffic.

#### `/Treatments/Clear-Braces/`
- **Why:** Premium option for aesthetics-minded; strong long-tail.
- **Conversion:** Alternative for non-Invisalign candidates.
- **SEO:** “clear braces Austin”, “ceramic braces Austin.”

#### `/Treatments/Retainers/`
- **Why:** Retainers program creates recurring revenue + protects outcomes.
- **Conversion:** Signup/appointment for retainer check.
- **SEO:** “retainers Austin”, “lost retainer Austin.”

#### `/Treatments/Bite-Correction/`
- **Why:** Many patients search by problem, not treatment.
- **Conversion:** Maps “symptom/problem” → treatment path.
- **SEO:** Long-tail dominance for: overbite/underbite/open bite/crossbite/crowding.

#### `/Treatments/TMJ-Bite-Concerns/` (Optional)
- **Why:** Only if clinic evaluates TMJ/bite-related concerns.
- **Conversion:** Captures high-pain urgency.
- **SEO:** “TMJ orthodontist Austin” (competitive; needs careful claims).

#### `/Treatments/Emergency-Orthodontist/` (Required)
- **Why:** High-intent urgent traffic (broken bracket, poking wire).
- **Conversion:** Call/text immediate; fast appointment.
- **SEO:** “emergency orthodontist Austin” and “broken braces Austin.”

### Patient-type pages (Required: Children / Teens / Adults)

#### `/Patients/Children-Orthodontics/` (Required)
- **Why:** Parents need reassurance + early ortho education.
- **Conversion:** “age 7 evaluation” + schedule consult.
- **SEO:** “kids orthodontist Austin”, “early orthodontics Austin.”

#### `/Patients/Teen-Orthodontics/` (Required)
- **Why:** Largest case volume segment.
- **Conversion:** Confidence messaging, braces/aligner options, school-friendly scheduling.
- **SEO:** “teen braces Austin”, “teen Invisalign Austin.”

#### `/Patients/Adult-Orthodontics/` (Required)
- **Why:** Invisalign-heavy, premium-friendly.
- **Conversion:** Discretion, efficiency, remote monitoring.
- **SEO:** “adult Invisalign Austin”, “adult orthodontist Austin.”

#### `/Patients/Invisalign-for-Teens/`
- **Why:** Dedicated Invisalign teen page improves conversion from teen queries.
- **Conversion:** Parent + teen reassurance; compliance tips.
- **SEO:** “Invisalign teen Austin.”

#### `/Patients/Invisalign-for-Adults/`
- **Why:** Dedicated adult Invisalign page improves match to intent.
- **Conversion:** Lifestyle fit + professional angle.
- **SEO:** “Invisalign for adults Austin.”

### New patient onboarding (Required)

#### `/New-Patients/New-Patient-Start-Here/` (Required)
- **Why:** Central onboarding hub reduces friction.
- **Conversion:** Step-by-step: book → scan → plan → start.

### Blog structure (Required)

#### `/Blog/` + categories (Required)
- **Why:** Content engine for SEO dominance and trust.
- **Conversion:** nurtures undecided visitors; retargeting; internal links to service pages.
- **SEO:** Topic clusters build authority for Invisalign + braces + cost + timelines.

**Recommended blog cluster model:**
- Hub pages = category pages
- Spokes = articles targeting one long-tail each
- Every article links to (a) relevant treatment page, (b) city/service page when relevant, (c) consultation CTA.

### City-based SEO landing pages (Required)

#### `/City/Orthodontist-Austin-TX/` (City hub)
- **Why:** Local relevance + maps synergy.
- **Conversion:** “near me” visitors get quick trust + location info.
- **SEO:** “orthodontist Austin TX” is a core head term.

#### `/City/Invisalign-Austin-TX/` and `/City/Braces-Austin-TX/`
- **Why:** Exact-match intent pages for core services.
- **Conversion:** “book Invisalign consult in Austin” style CTAs.
- **SEO:** Strong for high-intent “service + city” searches.

#### Neighborhood/suburb pages (South Lamar, Downtown, Round Rock, Cedar Park, West Lake Hills)
- **Why:** Expand capture without diluting Austin brand; each targets localized search behavior.
- **Conversion:** “closest office” + directions and scheduling.
- **SEO:** Neighborhood pages rank faster than city head terms and feed topical/local authority.

**Quality rule:** Each city/neighborhood page must be unique (local landmarks, driving directions, patient mix, FAQs). Avoid thin/duplicate content.

### Legal pages (Required)

#### `/Legal/Privacy-Policy/`, `/Legal/Terms-of-Use/`, `/Legal/Accessibility-Statement/`, `/Legal/Medical-Disclaimer/`
- **Why:** Compliance, credibility, ad platform requirements.
- **Conversion:** Indirect—reduces friction and increases trust.
- **SEO:** Minor; but protects reputation and supports E‑E‑A‑T.

## Internal linking strategy (hub-and-spoke + conversion routing)

### 1) Primary hubs
- **Invisalign hub:** `/Treatments/Invisalign/`
- **Braces hub:** `/Treatments/Braces/`
- **New Patient hub:** `/New-Patients/New-Patient-Start-Here/`
- **Austin city hub:** `/City/Orthodontist-Austin-TX/`

Each hub links to:
- Patient-type pages (Adults/Teens/Children)
- Financing + Reviews + Gallery
- Comparison page (Invisalign vs Braces)

### 2) Patient-type cross-links
- Children ↔ Teen pages cross-link
- Teen page links to Braces + Invisalign for Teens
- Adult page links to Invisalign for Adults + clear braces (if appropriate)

### 3) Blog topic clusters
- Every blog post links to:
  - 1 primary service page (Invisalign/Braces/Retainers)
  - 1 supporting proof page (Gallery or Reviews) occasionally
  - CTA to Contact / Schedule

### 4) City pages
- City pages link to:
  - Corresponding treatment hubs (Invisalign/Braces)
  - Location page + Contact
  - Reviews and Gallery

### 5) Conversion routing
Best-practice path examples:
- **High intent** (“Invisalign Austin”) → Invisalign hub → Financing → Book consult
- **Uncertain** (“Invisalign vs braces”) → comparison → recommended next step quiz/CTA → consult
- **Urgent** (“broken wire”) → Emergency page → call/text → same-day slot
- **Parent** (“kids orthodontist”) → Children page → What to expect → consult
